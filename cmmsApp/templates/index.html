OUR POLES & ACCESSORIES WORK PROCESS

Our structured approach ensures the highest standards of quality, safety, and operational integrity for all your transmission line material needs.

PROJECT ANALYSIS

We begin with a thorough consultation to define specifications, voltage requirements (e.g., 33kV to 1200kV), and environmental conditions for pole, wire, and insulator selection.

SOURCING & TESTING

We source reliable, high-integrity materials (poles, wires, OPGW, ADSS accessories) and conduct rigorous type testing to meet health, safety, and environmental standards.

LOGISTICS & DELIVERY

We manage the complete supply chain, ensuring all hardware, fittings, insulators, and anchoring accessories are delivered safely and on time to secure uninterrupted electrical supply.

SUPPORT & SERVICE

Our commitment continues with dedicated post-supply support and services, including provision for emergency restoration and modular structures when needed.
